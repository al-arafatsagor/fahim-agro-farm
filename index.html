The farm is established

I am a young entepreneur. This project was established in 2013. I started with only fish farming. Gradually, I have expanded into large-scale production of fish,livestock,poultry,eggs and milk etc. currently, we have created employment opportunities for some people and more are being created. We produce livestock feed naturally using organic methods. In addition, we have a dealership for animal feed. through which we sell feed not only for our own farm but also for other farms.

For any inquiries, fell free to contact us thanks.
